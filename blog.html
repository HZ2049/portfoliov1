Veronica Decides to Die By Paulo Coelho
Post By: Huzeyfa Seid
10-02-2025

Veronika Decides to Die isn’t just about a woman’s attempted suicide—it’s about rediscovering the will to live by finding personal meaning beyond societal scripts. For someone who values faith, purpose, and self-determination, this book serves as both a cautionary tale and an affirmation: without a higher purpose, even a "perfect" life can feel empty—but with meaning, even madness can be a form of freedom.
